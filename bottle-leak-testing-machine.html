Bottle Leak Testing Machine

A Bottle Leak Testing Machine is a specialized system designed to ensure the quality and integrity of bottles by detecting leaks, micro-holes, and structural defects. It is widely used in industries such as packaging, beverages, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals to maintain product safety and prevent leakage during storage and transportation.


This machine uses advanced air pressure or vacuum-based testing methods to accurately identify leaks without damaging the bottle. With high-speed operation and precision control, it is ideal for integration into automated production lines.

Key Features:
  • High precision leak detection using air pressure or vacuum technology
  • Fast cycle time with consistent and repeatable results
  • Non-destructive testing method ensuring product integrity
  • Suitable for various bottle sizes and materials (plastic, PET, glass)
Applications:
  • PET bottle testing in beverage industries
  • Pharmaceutical and chemical container inspection
  • Packaging quality control in manufacturing lines
