About Us

Rainbee Automation India Pvt. Ltd. was established with a vision to provide high-quality leak testing and automation solutions to modern manufacturing industries.

Our journey began with Rainbow Automotive Industries, where we gained strong expertise in fixture manufacturing, machining fixtures, and welding fixtures.

With evolving industry demands, we strategically transitioned from fixture manufacturing to:

  • Leak Testing Technology
  • Industrial Automation Solutions

Today, we combine our strong engineering foundation with advanced technology to deliver precise, reliable, and customized solutions.

Our focus remains on innovation, quality, and delivering leak-free confidence to our customers.
